About Computer Science Engineering



Raj Kumar

H.O.D. Computer Science Engineering Department

Raj Kumar is the Head of the Computer Science Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic Jamui. With a strong background in computer science and engineering, Raj Kumar has been instrumental in shaping the curriculum and fostering a culture of innovation within the department. His dedication to academic excellence and student success has made him a respected figure among both faculty and students. Computer Sc. & Engineering was established in the year 1989. The core branch has eminent, experienced and efficient faculties. State of the art world class labs with modern updated equipments, apparatus and machines is a great source of learning, having adequate facilities. The Computer Lab, Workshop, Drawing Halls. The intake capacity in this branch is of 60 students.
