Our History

Government Polytechnic Jamui is a premier technical institution located in Jamui, Bihar. Established in [Year], we are committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation in the field of science and technology. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in their careers and contribute to the development of society.

Our vision is to be a leading polytechnic institution recognized for excellence in education, research, and community engagement. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

At Government Polytechnic Jamui, we offer a wide range of programs in engineering and technology, including Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Our dedicated faculty members are experts in their fields and are committed to providing a high-quality education to our students.

We also have state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and a comprehensive library. Our campus is designed to foster collaboration and innovation, providing students with the resources they need to succeed.

We are proud of our rich history and the achievements of our alumni, who have gone on to make significant contributions in various industries and fields. We are committed to continuing our legacy of excellence and look forward to welcoming new students to our community.
